Home

Hi, I am a 3rd-year PhD student at the University of Queensland in Australia. I am expected to complete my PhD requirements by June 2025. I am currently working on large-scale cosmology. I use the fluctuations of the galaxy density (power spectrum) and the motion of galaxies (peculiar velocities) to constrain the cosmological parameters. I am a member of the Dark Energy Spectroscopic Instrument (DESI) survey, which will observe more than 40 million galaxies in 5 years. Recent results from DESI show that dark energy may evolve with time, contrary to our concordance cosmological model. I am interested in using galaxies' power spectrum and peculiar velocity to obtain tighter constraints on the time evolution of dark energy. Fork this repository to create your own site!
